## Highlights (Elsevier requires 3–5, each ≤ 85 characters)

- Public, dependency-free closed-loop benchmark for online truckload bid acceptance
- Operational feasibility (HOS, windows, yard delays) enters the reward, not a filter
- Lagrangian-per-truck bound is 20.7–39.3% tighter than the LP hindsight ceiling
- Surrogate-to-rollout cascade recovers ~98% of rollout profit at ~half the latency
- On the tight scenario the cascade is statistically indistinguishable from rollout
